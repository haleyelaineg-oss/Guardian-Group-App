Regulatory Reference Documents

As mentioned in the ISA Article, drivers hauling equipment are subject to federal cargo securement standards under 49 CFR Part 393. These regulations establish minimum requirements for tie-down working load limits, the minimum number of tie-downs based on cargo weight and dimensions, and en-route inspection requirements. For heavy vehicles and machinery specifically, requirements are outlined in § 393.130. Requirements vary based on what is being hauled. Knowing which standards apply to your specific equipment is part of operating responsibly and legally. Listed below are official FMCSA and DOT publications.
